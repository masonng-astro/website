About Me

I am Wei Chieh Ng (Mason) (he/his/him pronouns). I am currently a first-year graduate student at MIT's Department of Physics and the Kavli Institute for Astrophysics and Space Research. I mainly work with Professor Deepto Chakrabarty and Dr. Jack Steiner on X-ray timing analyses of neutron stars in the data. This website, like myself, are works in progress. You can learn more about me and my previous endeavors in the other sections of my website.
